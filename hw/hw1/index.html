Modern technology is always advancing throughout everyday. Just yesterday, Tesla made a historical achievenment by using reusable rockets to send a tesla roadster to mars
We, as a society utalize technology each and everyday for personal matters to finincal purposes. We rely on different networks to complete our transactions and communications.
However, certain individuals have realized the reward behind illegally gaining data from users. These indiviudals are known as hackers, and the purpose behind their intentions
is for revenge, money, and/or chaos. And these hackers are categorized as; White Hats, Black Hats, and Grey Hats.

  • White Hats: Has full permission from the company to hack the server to search for flaws in the security.
  • Black Hats: Has no permission from the company and hacks the server for malevolent personal gain.
  • No Hats/Grey Hats: Hacks the servers without any permission but notifies the company about their security flaws


Thus, the intentions of this webpage is to talk about some techniques these hackers will utalized to gain access. Some techniques I will be going over are the various types
of malware, DDoS, and a method of attack as known as a man-in-the middle attack.
